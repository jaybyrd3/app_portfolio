About Me

Hello! I'm Jimmy Byrd, a computer science student with a deep-rooted passion for programming.

My tech journey commenced in the world of video games. At a young age, I immersed myself in the world of modded Minecraft, where I dabbled in the Lua programming language within the Minecraft mod ComputerCraft. This early exposure to coding ignited my curiosity.

As life progressed, I found myself hosting Ark Survival servers and managing a vibrant community of over 12,000 members. The challenges of managing such a large community led me to teach myself Python, enabling me to programmatically interact with server host APIs. I developed a powerful Discord bot and began work on a web interface. It soon became apparent that programming had become my new passion, leading me to pursue higher education in computer science.

Although I shifted my focus away from game server hosting, I continued to support and advise my friends who took over the Discord project I initiated. It has now grown to serve over 700,000 Discord users. You can find the project here.

Since then, I've honed my skills in various programming languages, including:

  • Python
  • Java
  • SQL
  • HTML
  • Swift
  • C++

I've had the privilege to work on several exciting projects, including:

  • Developing Discord bots and websites for managing Nitrado hosted game servers
  • Creating this personal portfolio website
  • Designing an electronic checkerboard game with AI opponents
  • Making progress on a cross-platform social experiment app using Flutter
  • Advancing a game project utilizing Unreal Engine 5

Aside from my technical skills, I take pride in being a strong team player with exceptional problem-solving abilities.
